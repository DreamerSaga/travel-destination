Why Klaipėda?

Klaipėda - a city on the move

With its charming coastal vibe and lush green surroundings, Klaipėda is the ultimate destination for those seeking a laid-back lifestyle. You can enjoy the perks of a big city without the hustle and bustle of daily traffic jams, as everything is just a hop, skip and a jump away in this 15-minute city.

The city's Scandinavian climate brings you closer to nature, with the beach and forest always just a short distance away and more than 200 kilometres of bike lanes and a public transport network, making it a breeze to get around. You can explore the city's vibrant cultural scene, with festivals, exhibitions, and performances throughout the year.

Plus, Klaipėda's family-friendly atmosphere means there's always something fun and exciting for everyone to enjoy. Whether you're looking for a quick dip in the sea or a stroll through the park, Klaipėda has got you covered. Experience the ultimate work-life balance in Klaipėda – where work and vacations mix forever.

10 TILTŲ Krantas

10 Tiltų krantas - is a space for gourmet food, craft drinks and friends.

Address:

Vivalavita

It is a restaurant located 70 metres up on the 20th floor of the K-Centre of the Amberton Hotel Klaipėda. Guests of this lounge-style restaurant can enjoy not only a wide selection of exotic dishes and a wide range of cocktails, but also a magnificent panorama of the Curonian Spit, the harbour, and the Baltic Sea.

Address:

Naujojo Sodo str. 1A

Ararat

This luxurious restaurant with Armenian cuisine is the ideal venue for a business meeting, romantic dinner, private celebration or gala event. The restaurant’s intriguing chef’s suggestions will surprise even the most discerning palate, and the gourmet dishes will take on a special flavour when you taste the wine flown in directly from Armenia.

Address:

Liepų str. 48A

XIX amžius

Restaurant “XIX amžius” is a place where history and today meet. Beautiful surroundings, classic, cosy, good food and a great team. On Saturdays there are musical evenings with a saxophonist.

Address:

S. Neries str. 16A

STORAS KATINAS

The Fat Cat Gastrobar has remained one of the favourite destinations of tourists and local visitors for many years. Located in the heart of Klaipėda Old Town, just a few steps away from the Theatre Square. “Fat Cat” is a cosy place to enjoy a delicious meal or a few drinks with friends.

Address:

Žvejų str. 21
